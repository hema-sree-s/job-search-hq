Import from Resume

Upload your resume as a Word document (.docx) — not a PDF. Word documents extract far more reliably (real paragraphs and words, not just positioned pixels), so this pulls clean text into your Documents tab and auto-fills email/phone if found. It's a starting point, not a finished profile — review and edit after. Your file is also converted to a PDF and saved here (up to ~700KB) so you can re-download a polished copy later. If your resume is currently a PDF, open it in Word (or Google Docs → File → Download → Word) and export it as .docx first.
